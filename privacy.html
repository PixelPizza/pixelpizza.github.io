Privacy Policy

We are not liable if you share your discord password or token with us.

The discord data we collect is user ids, channel ids, guild ids and links to images of orders.

We collect this data, because we need it to identify which user ordered, cooked or delivered an order (user ids) and where to deliver that order to (guild and channel ids).

Aside from the discord data we collect we also store:

  • Your orders to be able to know what to make
  • The date of ordering, cooking and delivering. This is mostly used for complaints if an order takes too long to make
  • The way the order got delivered, this gets used for checking wether a new invite can be generated for an order if the old one was invalid
  • The reason why an order was deleted, so it can be recovered if it should not have been removed.
  • Delivery messages of deliverers for delivering the order with a custom message.

We do not share this data with anyone except discord and our workers.

If you have any concerns about our bot you can contact us via pixel-pizza@outlook.com or our support server.

To remove stored data, please contact us at pixel-pizza@outlook.com or in our support server and we will remove it for you.
